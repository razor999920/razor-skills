# deep-research

**Group:** Productivity & research

## What it does
A research harness that fans out web searches, fetches sources, adversarially verifies claims, and synthesizes a **cited** report.

## Triggers
A request for a deep, multi-source, fact-checked report on a topic.

## Note
Before running, it checks whether the question is specific enough. If it's underspecified (e.g., "what car to buy" with no budget/use-case/region), it asks 2–3 clarifying questions first, then folds the answers into the research.
