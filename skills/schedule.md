# schedule

**Group:** Productivity & research

## What it does
Creates or updates a task that runs automatically — either on a repeating cadence (every morning, weekly, hourly) or once at a specific future time (tomorrow at 3pm, in an hour).

## Triggers
"every day", "each morning", "remind me in an hour", "run this at noon", or rescheduling an existing automated task.

## Not for
One-off work you want done now. ("Summarize yesterday's emails" is a single task, not a schedule.)

## Note
Often offered proactively after something that naturally recurs — a briefing, digest, or status check.
